ABOUT HG LABOR

HG Labor is the official Minecraft server of German YouTuber NoRisk, featuring a unique HeroFFA Server custom mods.

The server experience is the best when using the Norisk Client.

With regular events, and updates, HGLabor stays up-to-date and exciting for its community.
